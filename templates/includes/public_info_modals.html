Privacy Policy

How GSO Request Management collects and uses data.

Last updated: April 2026

Information We Collect

We collect account profile details, request submission data, attachments, and service processing logs needed to deliver GSO services.

How We Use Information

Data is used to process service requests, coordinate units and personnel, generate reports, and improve response performance.

Data Access and Retention

Access is role-based and limited to authorized personnel. Request and audit data are retained according to institutional policy and legal requirements.

Terms of Service

Usage rules for all users of the GSO Request Management system.

Last updated: April 2026

Authorized Use

The system is for official service requests and related operations only. Users must provide accurate and complete information.

Account Responsibility

Users are responsible for securing their credentials and for all activity performed using their accounts.

Operational Limits

The GSO team may suspend misuse, perform maintenance, and update features to protect service quality and security.

Contact Support

Where to report issues or ask for assistance.

Last updated: April 2026

Help Channels

For account, request, or report issues, contact the GSO Office through your official office channel or assigned coordinator.

Include These Details

Share your request ID, a short issue description, expected result, and screenshots if available so support can respond faster.

Service Hours

Support follows regular office hours unless emergency handling is required by authorized units.
